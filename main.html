Welcome to Peddapalli District Collectorate!


Peddapalli, located in the heart of Telangana, is a district rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. It is known for its agricultural prominence, vibrant villages, and the spirit of its people. As a fast-growing district, Peddapalli plays a vital role in the state’s development and progress. The Peddapalli District Collectorate is committed to ensuring transparent, responsive, and efficient governance. We strive to serve the people by offering various services, managing resources, and fostering development across sectors including education, healthcare, infrastructure, and rural development.
